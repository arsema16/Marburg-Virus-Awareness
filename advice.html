If you have symptoms

Immediate steps

  • Stay isolated; use a separate room and bathroom if possible.
  • Seek medical care immediately.
  • Do not share personal items (utensils, towels, bedding).
  • Drink plenty of clean water or oral rehydration solutions; rest.

Monitor and report

  • Follow instructions from health professionals.
  • Report suspected illness to health authorities quickly.
  • Use protective measures while awaiting care.

What caregivers should do

Use protective equipment

Wear gloves, masks, and protective clothing when caring for someone.

Avoid contact with body fluids

Blood, vomit, saliva, sweat, and urine can spread the virus; only trained personnel should handle safely.

Clean surfaces with disinfectant

Clean floors, bedding, and surfaces with bleach or approved disinfectants.

Watch for symptoms

If exposed, monitor for fever, weakness, or other symptoms for 21 days and seek help if noticed.
